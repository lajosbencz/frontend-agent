# Descaling Solution

A citric-acid-based descaling solution formulated for espresso machines. It dissolves limescale from
boilers and water lines without the seal and odor problems that come from using household vinegar.
Descale on a schedule set by your [water quality](/brewcraft/docs/water-quality) — monthly on hard unfiltered
water, every 3-6 months on filtered — following [descaling & maintenance](/brewcraft/docs/maintenance-descaling).
Safe for every BrewCraft machine. One bottle is good for roughly 6-8 descales.
