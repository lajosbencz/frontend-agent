# 58mm Dosing Cup

A stainless cup sized to a 58mm basket. Grind into the cup (great with single-dose grinders like the
[Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder) or [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder)), then invert it onto the
portafilter for a clean, low-static transfer. Pairs naturally with a [WDT tool](/brewcraft/products/wdt-tool) and
[puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) routine.
