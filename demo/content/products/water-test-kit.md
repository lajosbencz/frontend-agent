# Water Hardness Test Kit

A pack of dip-strip tests for measuring your tap water's hardness (GH) and total hardness. Knowing your
water tells you how often to [descale](/brewcraft/docs/maintenance-descaling) and whether you need filtration —
the Specialty Coffee Association target is roughly 35-85 ppm calcium hardness. See
[water quality](/brewcraft/docs/water-quality) for what the numbers mean and how to adjust. Works for any machine.
