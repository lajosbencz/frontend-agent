# BrewCraft Pico

The Pico is our smallest and most affordable machine — built for someone pulling their first shots
who wants a low-risk way into espresso. It uses a single thermoblock (fast to heat, ~1 minute) and
ships with **pressurized (dual-wall) baskets** that are far more forgiving of grind and puck-prep
mistakes, so you can get a drinkable shot before you've learned to [dial in](/brewcraft/docs/dialing-in).

- Boiler: single thermoblock
- Portafilter: 58mm
- PID temperature control: no (fixed thermostat)
- Heat-up time: ~60 seconds
- Footprint: 20 × 25 cm

Because it has one heater, you brew first and then steam — expect a short wait to switch modes, like the
[Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine). When you outgrow the pressurized baskets, drop in a
[Precision Basket](/brewcraft/products/precision-basket) and a real [grinder](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders). Not
recommended for milk-heavy households making several drinks back to back — look at the
[Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine) or [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine) instead.
