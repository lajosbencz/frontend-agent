# 350ml Milk Pitcher

A 350ml (12oz) stainless steaming pitcher with a sharp, tapered spout for pouring
[microfoam](/brewcraft/docs/milk-steaming) and latte art. The 350ml size suits one to two drinks and pairs well
with steam-capable machines like the [Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine) and [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine).
Fill to just below the spout weld, and see [milk steaming](/brewcraft/docs/milk-steaming) for stretching and
texturing technique.
