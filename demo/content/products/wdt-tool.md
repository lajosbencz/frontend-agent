# WDT Distribution Tool

A set of fine (0.3mm) needles on a handle, used to stir the grounds in the basket after dosing. This
**Weiss Distribution Technique** breaks up clumps and evens out the coffee bed before tamping — for most
home baristas it's the single most effective fix for [channeling](/brewcraft/docs/troubleshooting). Works with any
grinder and machine. See [puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) for where WDT fits in the routine.
