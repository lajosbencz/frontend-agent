# 18g Precision Basket

A non-pressurized (single-wall) filter basket with laser-drilled, uniformly sized holes for even water
flow across the whole puck. Sized for an 18g dose — a common target for a double shot at a
[1:2 ratio](/brewcraft/docs/espresso-fundamentals). This is the upgrade from a pressurized starter basket once
you've got a real [grinder](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders) and can [dial in](/brewcraft/docs/dialing-in). Fits all
58mm machines; the entry [Pico](/brewcraft/products/pico-machine) can use it once you move off pressurized baskets.
