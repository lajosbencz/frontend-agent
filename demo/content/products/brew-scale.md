# Espresso Brew Scale

A compact scale with 0.1g resolution and a built-in timer, sized to fit under the portafilter on the drip
tray. Weighing your **dose** (grounds in) and **yield** (espresso out) is the foundation of repeatable
espresso — see [espresso fundamentals](/brewcraft/docs/espresso-fundamentals) and [dialing in](/brewcraft/docs/dialing-in).
Auto-start timing triggers on first drops. Works with every BrewCraft machine.
