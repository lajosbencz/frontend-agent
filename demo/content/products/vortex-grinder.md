# BrewCraft Vortex

The Vortex is a single-dose grinder with 64mm flat burrs and stepless adjustment. You weigh your beans,
drop them in, and grind directly into your portafilter or [dosing cup](/brewcraft/products/dosing-cup) — there's no
hopper and near-zero retention, so every dose is fresh and the beans in the machine never go stale.

- Burr type: flat, 64mm
- Adjustment: stepless
- Workflow: single dose (no hopper)
- Retention: <0.1g

Flat burrs give a more uniform grind and a cleaner, more articulate cup than a conical grinder — see
[grinder burrs](/brewcraft/docs/grinder-burrs). It's a step below the [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder) in
build and burr size but shares the single-dose, clarity-first philosophy. A great match for the
[Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine), [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine), or [Lever](/brewcraft/products/lever-machine).
