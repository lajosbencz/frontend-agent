# 58mm Tamper

A flat stainless-steel tamper machined to 58.4mm — a snug fit in a standard 58mm basket that helps you
tamp level and consistent. Level tamping is one of the biggest levers against
[channeling](/brewcraft/docs/troubleshooting); see [puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) for technique. Fits every BrewCraft
machine, all of which use 58mm portafilters.
