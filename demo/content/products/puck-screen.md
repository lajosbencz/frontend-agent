# 58mm Puck Screen

A 1.7mm stainless mesh screen that sits on top of the coffee puck. It keeps the group head's shower
screen clean (less backflushing needed) and can help even out how water first hits the bed. A minor but
popular upgrade — it won't fix [channeling](/brewcraft/docs/troubleshooting) on its own; good
[puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) still matters most.
