# Compact Grinder

A conical-burr grinder with a small footprint and a stepped adjustment ring — the safe, affordable choice
that works well with every machine we sell. Conical burrs are more forgiving when
[dialing in](/brewcraft/docs/dialing-in) than flat burrs, which makes this a great grinder to learn on.

- Burr type: conical, 40mm
- Adjustment: 40 stepped settings
- Hopper: 150g
- Footprint: small

If you want more clarity and stepless micro-adjustment later, step up to the flat-burr
[Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder) or [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder). For a no-electricity or
travel option, see the [Hand Grinder](/brewcraft/products/hand-grinder). Compare designs in
[grinder burrs](/brewcraft/docs/grinder-burrs).
