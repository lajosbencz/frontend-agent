# BrewCraft Flux

The Flux is a **heat-exchanger (HX)** machine: one large boiler held at steam temperature, with fresh
brew water drawn through a tube passing through that boiler on its way to the group. That means you can
steam milk and pull a shot at the same time — the big advantage over a single boiler — at a lower price
than a true [dual boiler](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine).

- Boiler: single heat-exchanger
- Portafilter: 58mm
- Steam power: high (large boiler)
- Simultaneous brew + steam: yes
- PID: steam pressure only

The trade-off is brew-temperature precision. After the machine sits idle the water in the HX tube
overheats, so the first shot needs a short **cooling flush** — run water through the group for 2-4
seconds until the temperature settles. See [Machine Types](/brewcraft/docs/machine-types) for how HX compares to
single- and dual-boiler designs. A great pick for latte/cappuccino drinkers who don't want to pay dual-
boiler prices.
