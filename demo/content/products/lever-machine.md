# BrewCraft Lever

The Lever is a spring-piston machine: you pull the lever down to charge a spring, and the spring drives
water through the puck as it releases. There is no electric pump. This gives a **natural declining
pressure profile** and gentle [pre-infusion](/brewcraft/docs/glossary), which many drinkers find produces a
sweeter, more forgiving shot.

- Mechanism: spring lever (no pump)
- Boiler: single, steam-capable
- Portafilter: 58mm
- Pressure: mechanical, naturally declining from ~9 bar
- Best for: hands-on enthusiasts who enjoy the ritual

The Lever rewards good [puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) and a consistent [grinder](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders) —
pair it with the [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder) or [Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder) grinder.
It has a learning curve and isn't the fastest for a morning rush, but it's the most engaging machine we
sell. A [bottomless portafilter](/brewcraft/products/bottomless-portafilter) makes it easy to watch your extraction
and catch [channeling](/brewcraft/docs/troubleshooting).
