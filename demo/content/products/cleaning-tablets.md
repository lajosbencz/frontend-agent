# Cleaning Tablets (50-pack)

Group-head cleaning tablets for **backflushing** — you place one in a blind basket and run the pump to
force detergent back through the group and three-way valve, dissolving coffee oils you can't reach from
outside. See [backflushing](/brewcraft/docs/backflushing) for the routine.

Only for machines with a three-way solenoid valve: the [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine),
[Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine), and [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine). **Do not** backflush the entry
[Pico](/brewcraft/products/pico-machine) (no solenoid valve) or the [Lever](/brewcraft/products/lever-machine) — a plain
water rinse is the right cleaning for those. Currently out of stock; restocking soon.
