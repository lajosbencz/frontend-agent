# BrewCraft Duo

The Duo is our dual-boiler machine: one boiler dedicated to brewing, one to steaming, each with its own
PID. You can pull a shot and steam milk at the same time with no waiting and no
[cooling flush](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine), and you can set brew temperature precisely for different
coffees — the biggest advantage over a [heat exchanger](/brewcraft/docs/machine-types).

- Boilers: 2 (independent brew + steam), both PID-controlled
- Portafilter: 58mm
- Simultaneous brew + steam: yes
- Brew temperature: adjustable, ±1°C stability
- Recommended for: daily multi-drink households

If you routinely make several lattes or cappuccinos back to back, the Duo's second boiler earns its
price. If you only ever make one drink at a time, you may not need it — the [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine)
is the better value. Pair with the [Precision Grinder](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder) for the finest
adjustment, or the [Compact Grinder](/brewcraft/products/compact-grinder) on a budget. See the
[compatibility guide](/brewcraft/docs/compatibility).
