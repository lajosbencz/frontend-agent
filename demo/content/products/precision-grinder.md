# Precision Grinder

Our flagship grinder: 83mm flat burrs with a stepless, high-resolution adjustment collar for the finest
espresso micro-adjustments. Single-dose workflow with near-zero retention. Built for someone who wants to
chase the last few percent of clarity and dial in precisely.

- Burr type: flat, 83mm
- Adjustment: stepless, high-resolution
- Workflow: single dose
- Retention: negligible

Flat burrs are less forgiving than conical — they reward good [puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) and a machine
with stable temperature. We recommend it for the [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine),
[Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine), or [Lever](/brewcraft/products/lever-machine). It's overkill for the entry
[Pico](/brewcraft/products/pico-machine) or a single-boiler-only [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine) setup, where the
[Compact](/brewcraft/products/compact-grinder) or [Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder) is a better-value match. See
[grinder burrs](/brewcraft/docs/grinder-burrs) and the [buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders).
