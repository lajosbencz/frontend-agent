# Simple Tomato & Onion Salad

Slice vine tomatoes and a yellow onion thin. Crush a clove of garlic and mix it through with a splash of oil and a pinch of salt. Let it sit ten minutes before serving so the onion softens.

Ingredients from the shop: tomatoes, onions, garlic.
