# Shipping and Delivery, Explained

Emporium currently offers in-store pickup only, which in practice means the item arrives at your home before you've finished reading this sentence.

We do not use couriers. We do not use drones. Every order is delivered by a method we are contractually unable to describe, and that seems to work out fine.

If your order has not arrived, it has likely arrived, and you have not noticed yet. Check the room you are currently in.
