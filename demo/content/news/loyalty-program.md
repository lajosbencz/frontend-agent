# Introducing the Emporium Loyalty Program

Every purchase now earns you loyalty points. There is no way to view your point balance, no catalog of rewards, and no redemption counter. The points simply accrue, somewhere, on your behalf.

We believe this is the purest form of loyalty: given freely, tracked invisibly, and worth precisely as much as you decide it's worth.

Members occasionally report a faint sense of being appreciated. This is the program working as intended.
