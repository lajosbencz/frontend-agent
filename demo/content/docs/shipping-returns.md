# Shipping And Returns

## Shipping

- **Free shipping** on orders over **$75**.
- Orders under $75 ship at a **$8 flat rate**.
- Orders leave our warehouse in **1-2 business days**.
- We currently ship **within the United States only**. This is a demo shop, so international delivery is not available.

You will receive tracking by email once your order ships.

## Returns

- Return any unused item in its **original packaging within 30 days** of delivery for a refund.
- Items must be unused and complete. Used grinders and machines that have brewed or ground coffee cannot be returned as new.
- Start a return from your order confirmation email, and we will send return instructions.

Consumable items such as [Cleaning Tablets](/brewcraft/products/cleaning-tablets) and [Descaling Solution](/brewcraft/products/descaling-solution) are only returnable if unopened.

## Warranty

| Category | Warranty |
|---|---|
| Machines and grinders | 2 years |
| Accessories | 1 year |

**Defective items are replaced free of charge**, including return shipping, for the duration of their warranty. Warranty covers manufacturing defects, not wear from normal use or damage from neglected [descaling](/brewcraft/docs/maintenance-descaling).
