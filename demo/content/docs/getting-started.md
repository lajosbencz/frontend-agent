# Getting Started With BrewCraft

Welcome to BrewCraft. We sell espresso **machines**, **grinders**, and the **accessories** that tie them together. If you are new to espresso, this page orients you before you buy.

## What a first setup needs

A complete first setup is three things:

- **A machine** to brew the shot.
- **A grinder** to grind fresh beans for it.
- **A tamper** to compress the puck evenly.

Add an [espresso brew scale](/brewcraft/products/brew-scale) as soon as you can. Weighing your dose and yield is the single biggest step toward repeatable shots.

## The grinder matters as much as the machine

Beginners often overspend on the machine and pair it with a cheap grinder. Grind quality and freshness shape extraction more than most machine features do. Split your budget deliberately. Read both guides before deciding:

- [Machine buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-machines)
- [Grinder buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders)

## Then learn the fundamentals

Gear is only half of it. Once your setup arrives, work through:

1. [Espresso fundamentals](/brewcraft/docs/espresso-fundamentals) - dose, ratio, and time.
2. [Dialing in](/brewcraft/docs/dialing-in) - adjusting the grind to taste.
3. [Puck prep](/brewcraft/docs/puck-prep) - distribution and tamping.
