# Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a grinder?**
Yes. Espresso depends on a fresh, even, adjustable grind, and pre-ground coffee stales within minutes and cannot be dialed in. A dedicated grinder is not optional. See the [grinder buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders).

**What size portafilter do BrewCraft machines use?**
All of them use **58mm**. That means tampers, baskets, and portafilters are interchangeable by size across the whole range. See [compatibility](/brewcraft/docs/compatibility).

**Can I steam milk and pull a shot at the same time?**
Only the [Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine) (heat exchanger) and [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine) (dual boiler) brew and steam simultaneously. Single-boiler machines like the [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine) need roughly 60-90 seconds to switch between brew and steam temperatures.

**Which machine should a beginner buy?**
The [Pico](/brewcraft/products/pico-machine) ($249) for the simplest, most affordable start, or the [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine) ($349) if you want PID temperature control and non-pressurized baskets to grow into. Compare them in the [machine buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-machines).

**How often should I descale?**
It depends on your water. With hard water, descale about monthly; with filtered or softened water, every 3-6 months. Test first with the [Water Hardness Test Kit](/brewcraft/products/water-test-kit) and read [water quality](/brewcraft/docs/water-quality) and [maintenance & descaling](/brewcraft/docs/maintenance-descaling).

**Can I backflush my machine?**
Only machines with a three-way valve: the [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine), [Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine), and [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine). The [Pico](/brewcraft/products/pico-machine) and [Lever](/brewcraft/products/lever-machine) cannot be backflushed. See [backflushing](/brewcraft/docs/backflushing).

**Which grinder goes with which machine?**
The [Compact](/brewcraft/products/compact-grinder) fits everything. The [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder) flagship shines on the Duo, Flux, and Lever but is overkill for the Pico and Solo. Full details in [compatibility](/brewcraft/docs/compatibility).
