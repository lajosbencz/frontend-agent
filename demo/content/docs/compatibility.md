# Compatibility Guide

Every BrewCraft machine uses a **58mm portafilter**, so tampers, baskets, screens, and portafilters are interchangeable across the range by size. The differences come down to which grinder makes sense and which accessories a machine's design supports.

## Machine matrix

| Machine | Recommended grinders | Notable accessories |
|---|---|---|
| [Pico](/brewcraft/products/pico-machine) $249 | [Hand](/brewcraft/products/hand-grinder), [Compact](/brewcraft/products/compact-grinder) | Ships pressurized baskets; no non-pressurized upgrade |
| [Solo](/brewcraft/products/solo-machine) $349 | [Compact](/brewcraft/products/compact-grinder), [Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder) | [Precision Basket](/brewcraft/products/precision-basket), [Bottomless Portafilter](/brewcraft/products/bottomless-portafilter), [Cleaning Tablets](/brewcraft/products/cleaning-tablets) |
| [Flux](/brewcraft/products/flux-machine) $549 | [Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder), [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder) | Precision Basket, Bottomless Portafilter, Cleaning Tablets |
| [Duo](/brewcraft/products/duo-machine) $649 | [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder), [Vortex](/brewcraft/products/vortex-grinder) | Precision Basket, Bottomless Portafilter, Cleaning Tablets |
| [Lever](/brewcraft/products/lever-machine) $799 | [Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder) | Precision Basket, Bottomless Portafilter |

## Grinder rules

- The **[Compact](/brewcraft/products/compact-grinder)** electric conical works with every machine and is the safe default.
- The **[Precision](/brewcraft/products/precision-grinder)** flat 83mm flagship is best on the **Duo, Flux, and Lever**, where the fine grind control is worth it. It is overkill for the **Pico and Solo**.
- The **[Hand](/brewcraft/products/hand-grinder)** is a low-cost entry match for the Pico.

See the [grinder buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-grinders) and [machine buying guide](/brewcraft/docs/buying-guide-machines) for the reasoning.

## Baskets and portafilters

The [Precision Basket](/brewcraft/products/precision-basket) and [Bottomless Portafilter](/brewcraft/products/bottomless-portafilter) fit the **non-pressurized 58mm machines: Solo, Flux, Duo, and Lever**. The **Pico** ships pressurized baskets and is not intended for non-pressurized upgrades.

## Backflushing and cleaning

Only machines with a three-way solenoid valve can be backflushed: the **Solo, Flux, and Duo**. The [Cleaning Tablets](/brewcraft/products/cleaning-tablets) are for those machines. The **Pico and Lever** are not backflushable. See [backflushing](/brewcraft/docs/backflushing).

## Works with anything

The [WDT Tool](/brewcraft/products/wdt-tool), [Knock Box](/brewcraft/products/knock-box), [Puck Screen](/brewcraft/products/puck-screen), [58mm Tamper](/brewcraft/products/tamper-58mm), [Brew Scale](/brewcraft/products/brew-scale), and [Water Hardness Test Kit](/brewcraft/products/water-test-kit) are compatible with every setup.
